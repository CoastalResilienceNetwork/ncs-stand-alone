Mitigation Potential for Select Pathways

The scientific analysis underlying this website considered 20 pathways. The 10 spatially explicit pathways presented here represent 75% of the total global NCS mitigation potential.

The 10 pathways not detailed on this website are: Improved (Forest) Plantations, (Forest) Fire Management, Avoided Coastal Wetland Impactsbeyond mangroves (seagrass and salt marsh), Coastal Wetland Restoration(mangroves, seagrass, and salt marsh), Avoided Grassland Conversion, Biochar, Cropland Nutrient Management, Conservation Agriculture, Trees in Croplands, Grazing –Improved Feed, Grazing –Animal Management.

The global data for these pathways were either not spatially explicit or were insufficient to disaggregate at the country level.
